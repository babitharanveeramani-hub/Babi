FAQ

Questions
answered.

How do I book a shoot?

Message me on WhatsApp (+91 9344813642) or Instagram (@smile_with_babi). Share your preferred date and session type, and I'll confirm availability and guide you through everything.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes — I'm based in Velankanni but open to traveling anywhere. Travel charges are calculated based on distance and discussed before booking.

How many edited photos will I receive?

Depending on the package: 10 photos for Quick Session, 25 for Signature Shoot, and 40 for Premium Shoot. Additional photos can be added at ₹200 each.

How long will delivery take?

Edited photos are typically delivered within 7–14 working days depending on the session type and current schedule.

Can I request extra photos or reels?

Yes — additional edited photos cost ₹200 per photo and highlight reels cost ₹1,000 per reel. The Premium package already includes 2 reels.
